# Project Purple Brand Direction

Project Purple is the working development name for a premium creator-first video platform.

The public name can change later. For now, Project Purple gives the product a clear internal identity while the app shell, visual system, and structure are built.

## Brand Position

Project Purple is not a YouTube clone.

It is a creator-first video platform focused on:

- Discovery
- Creator identity
- Collections
- Watch paths
- Premium presentation
- Clean viewing experiences
- Light and dark mode support

The platform should feel more curated and intentional than a general video warehouse.

## Brand Feeling

Project Purple should feel:

- Premium
- Modern
- Calm
- Creator-focused
- Polished
- Trustworthy
- Cinematic without being dark-only
- Social without being childish
- Powerful without being cluttered

Avoid:

- YouTube-red branding
- Gamer/neon overload
- Loud chaotic feeds
- Overcrowded recommendation rails
- Cheap SaaS dashboards
- Corporate stock-photo energy

## Color Direction

Primary palette:

- Purple
- Silver
- Black
- White

Purple is the main identity color. It gives the app a creative, premium, digital feel without copying YouTube.

Silver supports the premium feel. Black gives contrast and cinematic depth. White keeps the product clean and accessible.

## Theme Direction

Project Purple must support both light mode and dark mode.

Light mode should feel like the primary everyday experience:

- Clean
- Airy
- Premium
- Easy to browse
- Soft white and silver surfaces
- Purple accent moments

Dark mode should feel like cinema mode:

- Deep black/slate background
- Purple glow accents
- High contrast video surfaces
- Comfortable long-form viewing

## Product Language

Use language that creates distance from YouTube.

Preferred terms:

| Avoid | Use Instead |
| --- | --- |
| Channel | Creator Page |
| Playlists | Collections |
| Up Next | Watch Path |
| Subscriptions | Following |
| Library | Vault |
| Trending | Rising |
| Recommended | Featured |
| Creator Studio | Studio |
| Shorts | Clips |

## Tagline Concepts

Possible future public taglines:

- Your videos. Your audience.
- A cleaner home for independent video.
- Discover creators worth following.
- Video with a point of view.
- A premium space for creator-led video.

## Layout Identity

The layout should avoid the familiar YouTube skeleton:

- Do not default to a permanent full left sidebar.
- Do not default to a permanent right rail called Up Next.
- Do not build every page as endless rows of thumbnails.
- Do not bury creator identity under the video.

Instead, Project Purple should use:

- Top navigation
- Optional slim icon rail
- Creator spotlight modules
- Collections
- Watch Path modules
- Creator docks
- Large cinematic player areas
- Premium card layouts

## Main Experience Statement

Project Purple should feel like:

> A premium creator-first video platform where people discover creators, follow their work, and watch videos through curated collections and watch paths.

Not:

> A copy of YouTube with different colors.
